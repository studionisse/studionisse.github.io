About

Studio Nisse is an Omaha, Nebraska-based independent game studio founded January 29, 2018 by a family with a love of crafting and making memorable games for all ages.

Ian has been making video games since he was 10 on a Tandy 1000 using QBasic, and has worked professionally in the game industry since the 2000s. Jamie has been crafting creations her whole life, creating intricate paintings and strange physical artifacts. The two have been interested in merging the hand-crafted and digital worlds that they live in. They are supported by their 5 children, who provide both moral support and pointed criticism.
